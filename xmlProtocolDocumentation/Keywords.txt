"Single Line" Elements: <a />

    Access messages:
	sign - request new account
	cont - a contact that the user knows (only used in login and logout)

    Conversation messages:
	addPa - add a participant to a conversation (only used in updating a conversation)
	leave - remove a participant from a conversation (only used in updating a conversation)

    Contact messages:
	addCont - add a contact
	rmCont - remove contact
	udCont - update the contact information

Region Elements: <a></a>

	logout - When requesting log out
	udConv - Update the conversation information
	msg - sending a message

"Single Line" or Region Elements:

	login - when requesting log in
